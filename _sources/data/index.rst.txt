============
Data Service
============

Zolware Inc. provides a data service in the form of a web application where registered users can:

- Load, enter manually, or define through functions data sets in the form of time series or other.
- Store their data
- Stream their data
- Update their data from a streaming source
- Sell or share their data with other registered user
- Buy or share data from other registered users
- Vizualize, compare, analyze and predict their data
