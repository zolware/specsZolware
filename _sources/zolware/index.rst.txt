===================
Zolware's Structure
===================

Zolware Inc. propose three different services:

1. A free API to process, analyse, and predict data
2. A pay-per-use data service (web application) that allows registered users to define, stream, update data, buy, sell and share as well as vizualize and process their data through the API.
3. A customized service with dedicated solutions in forms of web applications.
